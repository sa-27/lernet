Thank you for your interest in Artemis 2020!

Your application has been received. Once we have received your application, essay, and both recommendations, we will send you an email confirming that your application is complete. If you don't receive a confirmation from us, please check with your teachers to ensure they have submitted a recommendation for you. Teachers will receive a confirmation once they submit their recommendation, so you could always ask them to forward this email receipt to you. We must be in receipt of all your materials by May 1st in order for you to be considered for Artemis.

 

All applicants will be notified about acceptance no later than May 22nd.


In the meantime, don't hesitate to email artemis@bu.edu if you have any questions!
