* Topic
You have just been hired by the BU Invention Department! They want you to create an invention to help address problems that people face. This could range from an everyday problem to one on a global scale. Describe why the problem you want to solve is important to you, what your invention is and how it will work, and how it will target the problem effectively. (If you're stuck, click here for some great ideas!)

Tell us about a particular homework assignment, project, paper, or production you have worked on that means something to you. What made it important or worthwhile for you? Did you work alone or with a group, and what responsibilities did you have? Did any challenges come up along the way, and if so, how did you resolve them?
